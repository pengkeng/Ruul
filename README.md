## What is Uul? ##

Uul is *Fuzzy* *Dynamtic* *Clustering* *Analysis* *Picture* *Tool* developed by
Tokhta Horqin at China University of Geosciences in China.

"Uul" (pronounced Ah-Goo-La, spelling уул and ᠠᠭᠤᠯᠠ) means "Mountain" in Mongolian.
